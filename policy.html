A

Shipping & Returns — No Return Policy

📦 Summary: EVNIKA Store maintains a No Return Policy for most inventory. Once delivered and accepted, returns are not accepted except in specific circumstances listed below. Please review product details carefully before ordering.

A.2 Scope

This policy applies to all standard retail purchases on our website. It covers all product categories unless explicitly marked "eligible for returns" on the product page. This includes intimate apparel, personalised items, perishable goods, and clearance items.

A.3 Limited Exceptions

We accept returns or exchanges in these limited cases:

  1. Manufacturing defects reported within 7 calendar days of delivery.
  2. Wrong item shipped (different SKU than ordered).
  3. Item damaged in transit (visible damage upon delivery).
  4. Items covered by an explicit seller or brand warranty including return rights.

To request a return under these exceptions, contact psree97palagatt@gmail.com within the stated timeframe with required documentation.

A.4 Required Documentation

  1. Your order number and date of purchase.
  2. Name and phone number used for the order.
  3. Description of the issue and resolution requested.
  4. At least two clear photographs of the problem and the shipping label (if damaged).

A.5 Inspection & Approval

Upon receipt of your claim, we may ask you to ship the item back for inspection. We'll provide a prepaid label only when the return is due to our error. Otherwise, return shipping costs may be deducted from any refund.

A.6 Timeline

We acknowledge claims within 3 business days and complete inspections within 10 business days.

A.7 Refunds & Exchanges

If approved, refunds are processed within 7–14 business days of receiving returned goods. Refund amounts may be reduced by restocking or handling fees disclosed during the approval process.

A.8 Non-Refundable Items

Even when a return is accepted, the following are non-refundable: final sale items, items missing original packaging, washed or used items, items with tampered seals, and gift cards.

A.9 Cancellation Policy

You may cancel within 1 hour of placing an order. After that window, orders in fulfillment cannot be cancelled and are subject to the No Return Policy unless an exception applies.

B

Refund Policy

B.1 When Refunds Are Issued

Refunds are only issued when a return is approved under the limited exceptions above, or if we are unable to deliver the goods. Refunds may be issued as:

  • Credit to the original payment method (preferred)
  • Store credit or gift voucher (only with customer consent)
  • Replacement item (exchange) where appropriate

B.2 Refund Calculation

Refunds reflect the item price paid, minus any applicable deductions such as shipping fees, restocking charges, or handling costs that were disclosed during the return approval process.

B.3 Processing Time

Once approved, refunds are initiated within 7–14 business days. The time to reflect in your account depends on your bank or payment processor.
